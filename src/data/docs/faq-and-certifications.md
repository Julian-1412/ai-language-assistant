# FAQ and Certifications

## Certifications
The academy issues a completion certificate for students who complete the program and attend at least 80% of classes.

## Placement test
New students can take a placement test before enrollment.

## Class size
Group classes have a maximum of 12 students.

## Customer support
Human support is available Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

## Scope limitation
The academy documents do not include visa guidance, job placement, or accommodation services.
