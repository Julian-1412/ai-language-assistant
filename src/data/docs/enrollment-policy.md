# Enrollment Policy

Students can enroll online or on-site.

## Enrollment steps
1. Fill out the registration form.
2. Choose the program and schedule.
3. Make the payment.
4. Send proof of payment by email.

## Required documents
- Copy of ID
- Proof of payment

## Contact
For enrollment support, contact advisors@languageacademy.co

## Refund policy
Payments are refundable only within the first 3 calendar days after enrollment, as long as the course has not started.
